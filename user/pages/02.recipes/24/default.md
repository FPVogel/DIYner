<h1>Prize-Winning Meat Loaf</h1>
<a href="http://www.cookbooks.com/Recipe-Details.aspx?id=923674/">Click here for the original source</a>
<h2>Ingredients</h2>
<p></p>
<p>1 1/2 lb. ground beef</p>
<p> </p>
<p>1 c. tomato juice</p>
<p> </p>
<p>3/4 c. oats (uncooked)</p>
<p> </p>
<p>1 egg, beaten</p>
<p> </p>
<p>1/4 c. chopped onion</p>
<p> </p>
<p>1/4 tsp. pepper</p>
<p> </p>
<p>1 1/2 tsp. salt</p>
<p></p>
<h2>Directions</h2>

<p></p>
<p>Mix well.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Press firmly into an 8 1/2 x 4 1/2 x 2 1/2-inch loaf pan.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Bake in preheated moderate oven.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Bake at 350\u00b0 for 1 hour.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Let stand 5 minutes before slicing.</p>
<p> </p>
<p>Makes 8 servings.</p>
<p></p>
